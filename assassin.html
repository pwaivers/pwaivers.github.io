Tribe Assassin Game

Welcome to Tribe Assassin - April 27, 2020

I am your handler, Linus. Welcome back to Assassin. The prize is a $50 Amazon gift card. However, this time the prize will be split between two players: the last the player standing and the player with the most kills. The last player standing will still be the WINNER, but the player with the most kills will split the prize money with the winner. If there is a tie for most kills, then the tie-breaker will be the player that survived the longest.

Here are the general rules:

  1. The game begins with each participant being assigned a target for assassination.
  2. With each kill, the successful assassin moves on acquiring their victim's assigned target, and the victim eliminated from the Game.
  3. A target may be killed using "hand-to-hand" or "weapons".
    • Hand-to-hand kills are accomplished by tapping a target on the shoulder.
    • Weapons include balls of paper, rolled up cloth, foam projectiles, and other soft projectiles.
  4. For a kill to be valid, both players have to be in the fair play area. I.e. you cannot throw a projectile from inside the wfh area to hit a player outside the wfh area.
  5. You may kill your assassin in self defense, called a self-defense kill. Doing so eliminates them from the game.
  6. Any kill that is not your target or a self-defense kill is called an unsanctioned kill. Unsanctioned kills will lead to more assassins being assigned you as a target.
  7. Note: Unsanctioned kills do count towards your total kill count for the "most kills" victory.
  8. The game ends when only one assassin is left alive. There is no time limit.

Play Area

The play area is inside Tribe Folsom only. Fair play areas include, but are not limited to:

  • Kitchen
  • 3rd floor sink room
  • Hallways
  • The living room where the couches are, but not wfh area
  • Movie room
  • Basement

The following areas are explicitly out of bounds:

  • The working area. The room starts at the Japanese fake walls
  • Any bedroom
  • Any bathroom
